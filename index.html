Scone Recipe
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
    1. Preheat oven to 425 F
    2. Whisk flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder
    3. Kneed in butter to flour mixture
    4. In a seperate bowl, whisk togeter milk, egg, and vanilla
    5. Save 2 tbsp of milk mixture to egg wash scones
    6. Add milk mixture to flour mixture. Kneed until smooth
    7. Roll out dough to one inch thickness
    8. Cut into desired shapes
    9. Brush scones with egg wash
    10. Bake in lined baking sheet for 13-15 mintues
